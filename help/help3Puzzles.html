Puzzles

What'll you find?

~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~

Sometimes, you'll find pages with puzzles. They can be any difficulty, and usually ends up giving you a code or a page link. These codes and links, given by a puzzle, will lead to a secret page. Keep your eyes peeled, as puzzles might be hidden in plain sight!

~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~

Secret pages are pages that you can't find normally like brute forcing an address or finding a hyperlink for it out in the open. They have a code that changes in length and complexity, just to prevent brute-forcing.

~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~

Most times, a puzzle might contain multiple solutions, in those cases, it will tell you how many there are, as well as how difficult your solution was to find. Usually, the hardest solution for a puzzle will contain a hyperlink to another secret page or another puzzle!

~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~

Here's a puzzle to get you started:

8345903134426541
The string of numbers can be reordered;
Some of the digits may be deleted;
Your result is a code.

Write them down as an address as such:
/secrets/QwtY/code.html
(e.g., /secrets/QwtY/12345.html)
