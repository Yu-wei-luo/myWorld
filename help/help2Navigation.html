Navigating this place is one of the most fundamental things to do, for most people, this will be trivial, but others might need it. If you feel like you know better, you can skip this and any others book you want. Guidance is for those who need and want it.

~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~

The most basic and simple way to navigate is through hyperlinks. You should be familiar with them, as they are what you used to arrive here. They usually look like this, though sometimes they might look like this. Those are hidden buttons, you'll have to keep your eyes peeled for them.

~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~

Another way to travel through this place is by manually typing in page names. To do this, go to your adress bar. You should be seeing something like this:

"https://yu-wei-luo.github.io/ bobaWebsite/help/ help2Navigation.html"

~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~=~

This is what you'll be rewriting to forcefully inject yourself into pages. Specifically the part that reads: "help/help2Navigation.html". The very last part after the last slash is the name of the page. It's usually something similar to "name.html". Every other part are called page folders. For example, the previously mentioned page was in the folder "help", and is called "help2Navigation.html". If you want to navigate to certain pages, you'll have to use this method, and you'll have to make sure that the page name and it's folders are correctly written, else it won't work!
